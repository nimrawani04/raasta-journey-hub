# 🚀 RAASTA — Your AI Companion for Life & Livelihood

> *"An AI assistant that helps you understand documents, grow your crops, access schemes, and get answers — all by voice, in your language."*

---

## 🏆 What is RAASTA?

**RAASTA** is not three apps. It is **ONE intelligent assistant** with three superpowers — built for the people who need it most.

Where most AI tools serve those who already have access, RAASTA serves those who don't. Rural farmers. First-generation document readers. People who speak Urdu and Kashmiri, not English.

This is AI for the **next billion**.

---

## 🧩 The Three Superpowers

---

### 📄 1. Samjho — Understand Any Document
*Powered by HAQQ*

**The problem:** A government notice arrives. Nobody in the village understands it. A deadline is missed. Rights are lost.

**What Samjho does:**
- User photographs any document (notice, form, letter)
- AI reads it via OCR
- Explains it in simple, spoken Urdu or Kashmiri
- No literacy required. No lawyer needed.

**Demo line:**
> *"This notice means you need to submit your land records by the 15th. Here's how…"*

**Why it hits:** It's emotional. It's a moment of dignity — someone finally understanding something that was always hidden behind language.

---

### 🌱 2. Zameen — Crop & Farming Help
*Powered by WADI*

**The problem:** A farmer sees spots on his apple leaves. He doesn't know if it's fungal, bacterial, or just the weather. He guesses wrong. He loses the harvest.

**What Zameen does:**
- User photographs a leaf or crop
- AI detects disease using vision model
- Explains treatment in local language
- Shows current mandi (market) prices

**Demo line:**
> *"Your apple crop shows early signs of fungal infection. Apply copper-based spray within 3 days. Current mandi price in Sopore: ₹42/kg."*

**Why it hits:** It's visual. It's immediate. It saves a season.

---

### 🎤 3. Raah — Voice Assistant
*The glue that holds everything together*

**The problem:** Typing is a barrier. Navigating apps is a barrier. Language is a barrier.

**What Raah does:**
- User speaks in Urdu or Kashmiri
- Whisper transcribes the voice
- LLM processes and answers
- TTS responds in natural speech

**Ask anything:**
- *"Mujhe PM Kisan scheme ke baare mein batao"*
- *"Is mahine mein seb ki fasal ke liye kya karoon?"*
- *"Yeh kagaz kyaa kehta hai?"*

**Why it hits:** It makes the whole product feel alive. Human. Intelligent.

## ⚠️ What NOT to Build

| ❌ Don't | ✅ Do |
|---|---|
| Multiple language toggle screens | Auto-detect from voice |
| User accounts / login | Open and instant |
| Complex onboarding | Zero friction home screen |
| 10 features per module | One core feature per mode |
| Fancy animations everywhere | One great mic interaction |

**Limit. Ship. Demo. Win.**
